Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Information

Helping ensure we deliver your products efficiently and keep you informed throughout the process.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after receiving your order confirmation email.

Shipping Methods & Costs

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days)
  • Free Shipping on orders over $100
  • Expedited Option, price calculated at checkout

International Shipping

  • We offer shipping globally via major carriers.
  • Customs duties and taxes calculated at checkout.

Returns

  • 30 day return on unused items.
  • Items must be unused, in original condition, and with tags attached.

Returns Process

  1. Contact our Customer Service at support@britiumgallery.com with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Receive your return authorization and instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely, and include your return authorization inside the package.
  4. Refund issued via the original payment method within 7 business days of receipt of the item.
