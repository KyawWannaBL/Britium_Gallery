Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and the Britium Gallery shopping experience.

Are Britium Gallery products authentic?
Yes. All products curated by Britium Gallery are carefully selected to meet our standards of quality, craftsmanship, and authenticity.
Do you offer international shipping?
International shipping options are available for selected locations. Shipping availability and fees will be displayed at checkout.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking number via email. You may also track your order using the Track Order page.
What is your return policy?
Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Visit the Return Request page to initiate.
How do I contact customer support?
You may reach our concierge team via the Contact page or through the live chat available on our website.
