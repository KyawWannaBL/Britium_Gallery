FAQs

Welcome to our Frequently Asked Questions page. Here you'll find answers to common queries about our products, orders, shipping, and more.

Orders

How do I place an order? +
You can order a product by adding items to cart and completing the checkout process.
Can I modify or cancel my order? +
Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting customer support.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Products

Are the products authentic? +
All products are guaranteed to be 100% authentic.
How do I care for my handbag? +
Keep your bag in a dust bag when not in use and avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight.
Will sold-out items be restocked? +
We restock popular items periodically. You can sign up for notifications on the product page.

Shipping

What are the shipping options? +
We offer a variety of domestic and international shipping options including standard and express delivery.
Do you ship internationally? +
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide.
How can I track my order? +
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email.

Returns

What is your return policy? +
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for items in their original condition.
