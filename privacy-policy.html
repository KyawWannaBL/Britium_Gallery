Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy of Britium Gallery. Protecting your personal information is important to us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Information related to purchase transactions and order history.
  • Web Usage Data including IP address, browser type, and page navigation statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Processing transactions and delivering orders efficiently.
  • Improving our Website, products, and customer services.
  • Communication regarding promotions, updates, and marketing materials (if opted in).
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respectfully implement personal information protection. We do not sell or rent your personal data to outside parties except as necessary for service providers.

In these cases, we may share data with payment processors and shipping companies to fulfill your orders. We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security

We strictly implement responsible measures to protect your personal data. We use encryption (SSL) to secure servers and ensure that personal information is transmitted safely. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, update, or request deletion of your personal information.
  • Object to the processing of your data under certain circumstances.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications by following instructions provided in emails.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze site traffic, usage, and facilitate user preferences for a better browsing experience.

7. Changes to This Policy

Britium Gallery reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
